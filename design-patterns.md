# 10 common design patterns

> Source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tv-_1er1mWI

- We separate design patterns to 3 types:
	- Creational
	- Structural
	- Behaviorial

- Creational:
	- Singleton
	- Prototype
	- Builder
	- Factory
- Structural:
	- Facade
	- Proxy
	- Iterator
- Behavioral:
	- Observer
	- Mediator
	- State
